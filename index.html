Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide quality education and holistic development that prepares students for life beyond the classroom. We envision a community of learners who are equipped with the skills, values, and knowledge to become responsible citizens and leaders.

Our History

San Agustin Elementary School has a rich history of excellence in education, founded on the values of integrity, respect, and commitment to the community. Since its establishment, the school has nurtured generations of leaders and citizens who contribute to the advancement of society.
