Welcome to San Agustin Elementary School

San Agustin Elementary School is committed to providing a nurturing environment where students can grow academically and personally. Our mission is to foster a love of learning and prepare students for the ultimate success in life.

About San Agustin Elementary School

San Agustin Elementary School has been a beacon of academic excellence for over 50 years. Our school is dedicated to providing a safe and nurturing environment for children to grow intellectually, socially, and emotionally. We believe in fostering creativity, critical thinking, and a strong sense of community in our students.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To develop young minds into responsible, compassionate, and confident individuals who will contribute positively to society.

Mission: To provide a nurturing environment where students can grow academically, socially, and emotionally while fostering creativity, critical thinking, and a strong sense of community.

Admissions

Welcome to the admissions page of San Agustin Elementary School! We are excited that you are considering us for your child’s education. Here is a brief overview of the admission process:

Admission Requirements:

  • Completed Application Form
  • Original Birth Certificate of the applicant
  • Recent 2x2 Photo
  • Proof of Residence (e.g., utility bill or rental agreement)
  • Immunization Record (for new students)

How to Apply:

  1. Step 1: Download the application form from our website or pick it up at the school's admissions office.
  2. Step 2: Fill out the application form with complete and accurate information.
  3. Step 3: Submit the completed application form along with the required documents to the admissions office.
  4. Step 4: Attend the admissions interview (for new students).
  5. Step 5: Once your application is accepted, you'll be notified to proceed with payment of the tuition fee and other necessary formalities.

If you have any questions or need assistance during the application process, please do not hesitate to contact our Admissions Office. We are here to help!
